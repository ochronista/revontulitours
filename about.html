Who are we?

We are RevontuliTours, a company offering world-class experiences in and around Rovaniemi, the heart of Finnish Lapland (and Santa Claus's town).

View our tours

Our mission

We aim to provide authentic tours while preserving the environment and cultures of Lapland.

We partner with local-owned companies to provide our tours, we hire local guides to train our staff to comply with Sámi, Finnish, and European Union costumes and regulations, and also seek to develop a sustainable tourism atmosphere in Lapland.

Why travel with us?

We focus on having small groups (up to 8 passengers per tour), so we can offer the best experience possible.

Beyond that, we operate with three core principles to guarantee the satisfaction of our customers in every tour:

  • > Experienced guides leading the tours
  • > Surroundings acknowledgement guiding the actions
  • > Sustainable practices driving the company

What is Revontuli?

According to old Finnish tales, the Northern Lights are made when the Revontuli (Fire Fox) hits its tail in the snow.

We are inspired by this tale and believe that, such as the Revontuli, we are able to enjoy the beauties of Lapland while mantaining a fun and relaxed environment, especially for our guests.
